Timing is expressed in engine tics. One tic is 28.57 ms; Doom runs this widget at 35 tics per second.

Vanilla accuracy note Original PC Doom has no blink sprite or blink branch. Its three idle sprites look center, left, and right. This archive keeps that behavior instead of inventing a blink; the shipped OUCH sign bug is documented separately from the intended reaction.
