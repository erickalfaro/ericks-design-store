Living dither

A gallery of moving pictures — spirits, storms, seas, and galaxies. Each piece is pure math writing a continuous field every frame — breathing entities, figures of flame and light, oceans, lightning, black holes — rendered through the same ordered Bayer 4×4 dither dither-avatar uses: one ink, on or off. ← → switch · click the canvas to reseed.

Each piece writes a continuous value per cell; the dither turns that into one ink color on/off per pixel via value > (BAYER[y%4][x%4] + 0.5) / 16. The art is in the field, not the palette.
